Biography

Driven by
Data & Curiosity

I'm a Senior Data Analyst with 5+ years of experience helping businesses unlock the full potential of their data. My journey started in financial analytics, where I discovered the transformative power of a well-crafted dashboard — and I've been obsessed with data storytelling ever since.

I specialize in bridging the gap between raw, complex datasets and strategic business decisions. Whether it's building a real-time Power BI dashboard for a C-suite executive or training a machine learning model to predict customer behavior, I bring both technical rigor and business intuition to every project.

When I'm not buried in data, I contribute to open-source analytics tools, mentor junior analysts, and explore how emerging AI technologies are reshaping the analytics landscape.
